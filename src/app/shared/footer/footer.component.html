About us
Masissneun came from the Korean word (mas-ssineun eumsig) means delicious food. Masissneun is a word of Hangul. Masissneun restaurant inspired by Korean culture where K-POP has been adopted. Named of their menu’s and drinks came from the famous K-POP group and celebrity.
